# 🐱 MeowAssistant - Add useful controls to your device

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release-blue)](https://github.com/Microstomuskittcommunityofinterests433/MeowAssistant/releases)

MeowAssistant adds custom quick settings tiles to your Android device. These tiles let you toggle system features with a single tap from your notification shade. The application targets rooted devices to grant the necessary permissions for deep system integration.

## 🛠 Prerequisites

You need a device with root access to use MeowAssistant. Root access provides the software with the permission it needs to modify system settings. If you do not have root access, the application will not function. Ensure you maintain your root management tool, such as Magisk or KernelSU, and verify that your device shows as rooted in your security settings.

You also need a Windows computer to install the management tool. While the primary application runs on your phone, the setup process uses your computer to bridge the connection and refine the installation.

## ⚙️ Installation Process

Connect your phone to your computer using a data cable. Ensure your phone is in USB Debugging mode. You can enable this in your phone settings under Developer Options. 

Open the software on your Windows computer. The user interface guides you through the process of connecting your device. Grant the application permission to access your device when your phone prompts you.

Once the computer software recognizes your phone, click the install button for the mobile application. The tool pushes the app to your phone automatically. Open the MeowAssistant app on your home screen to finalize the setup. 

## 🛡 Granting Root Access

When you open MeowAssistant for the first time, your root manager will send a notification asking for permission. Choose the "Grant" or "Allow" option. This step gives the app permission to modify your Quick Settings panel. 

The app includes a diagnostic page to confirm that the root access works correctly. If the screen shows a green checkmark, you have successfully set up the connection.

## 📱 Using Quick Settings Tiles

The main dashboard allows you to select which tiles you want to add to your notification panel. Tap the toggle button next to each feature to enable it. 

After you toggle a feature, swipe down from the top of your screen to open your Quick Settings panel. Tap the pencil icon at the bottom of the panel to enter edit mode. Look for the MeowAssistant tiles in the available list. Drag and drop your preferred tiles into your active panel layout. 

Now, tap these tiles to activate the controls instantly. You can change these at any time by returning to the MeowAssistant app.

## ❓ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If a tile does not work, verify that you granted root access. Open your root manager app and check the list of granted permissions. Make sure MeowAssistant is on that list.

If your computer does not see your device, check your USB cable. Use a high-quality data cable rather than a charging-only cable. You might also need to install universal driver software for your specific phone model on your computer.

Restart your phone and your computer if the connection hangs. Software conflicts often resolve after a reboot. For advanced errors, remove the app via your phone settings and repeat the installation steps.

## 📝 Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need root access for quick settings?
Root access allows the app to bypass standard system security restrictions. Without it, the Android operating system prevents third-party apps from adding buttons to the Quick Settings panel.

Does this app drain my battery?
MeowAssistant remains dormant when you do not interact with the tiles. It only runs code when you tap a button. It does not perform background tasks that consume power.

Can I install this on a computer without a phone?
No. The software requires an Android device to function. The desktop tool serves only as a helper for the installation process.

Who maintains this project?
The Microstomuskitt community develops and manages this tool. We focus on utility and stability for rooted device users. We track all changes through our repository history.

What happens if I remove root access?
The application will cease to function. The quick settings tiles will vanish or become unresponsive. Use your root manager to reinstate access if you wish to use the app again.

Is my data safe?
MeowAssistant does not collect personal information. It operates locally on your device. We do not transmit your settings or usage patterns to any external servers. The app only requires access to the system settings it manages.

Are there plans for new features?
We add support for new system toggles based on user requests. Check the main page for new releases. Feel free to suggest features that help you manage your rooted device more efficiently.
